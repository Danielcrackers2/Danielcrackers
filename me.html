1. PREAMBLE AND ESTABLISHMENT

Horizon Trust SACCO is a legally constituted, member-centric financial cooperative, founded upon mutual self-help and collective economic empowerment. It cultivates a disciplined savings culture and delivers accessible, affordable credit. The SACCO operates with transparency, cooperative principles, and full alignment with Uganda’s regulatory framework.

VISION
To stand as a preeminent, trusted, and innovative savings and credit institution, empowering members through sustainable financial growth, absolute transparency, and broad-based economic inclusion.

MISSION
To provide meticulously secure savings avenues, affordable and responsible loan products, and sound financial stewardship, strengthening economic stability and holistic development of all members.

11. OFFICIAL SACCO CONSTITUTION

ARTICLE 1: NAME

Horizon Trust SACCO (“the SACCO”).

ARTICLE 2: PURPOSES AND OBJECTIVES

a) Mobilize member savings. b) Extend prudent credit at equitable rates. c) Promote financial literacy & economic empowerment. d) Foster responsible financial management. e) Operate on cooperative principles, free from discrimination.

ARTICLE 3: MEMBERSHIP

3.1 Eligibility: Open to individuals committed to regular saving, active participation, and policy compliance.
3.2 Rights: Democratic participation, access savings/credit, transparent information, elect & be elected.
3.3 Responsibilities: Uphold integrity, meet savings obligations, timely loan repayment, attend meetings.

ARTICLE 4: GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE

a) General Assembly (supreme). b) Executive Committee. c) Standing and ad-hoc committees as established.

ARTICLE 5: FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION

a) All flows receipted & documented. b) Designated bank account. c) Monthly internal audits, annual external audit. d) Loan limits per committee & General Assembly.

ARTICLE 6: DISCIPLINARY FRAMEWORK

Grounds: fraud, willful default, misappropriation, gross disrespect. Measures: censure, fines, suspension, termination.

ARTICLE 7: AMENDMENTS

By two-thirds majority vote of General Assembly members present and voting.

ARTICLE 8: DISSOLUTION

After satisfying liabilities, remaining net assets distributed proportionally per member’s recorded savings balance.
